About Me

My name is Brent Nguyen and I am currently a senior at San Diego State University Studying Business Administration with an emphasis in Management Information Systems.

My life's purpose is fueled by a passion for innovation, primarily aimed at contributing significantly to society through technological advancements. This driving force was inspired by my grandma's compassionate actions towards Vietnamese immigrants adapting to life in San Diego post-Vietnam War. Witnessing the profound impact of her efforts, I've learned the importance of leading a fulfilled life is through helping others. My skills in technology, organization, communication, and leadership further underscore your commitment. These attributes are not only evident through my active participation in student organizations but also through various roles outside the academic environment.

On the other hand, some of my hobbies are playing sports like tennis and volleyball. I also love watching anime with titles such as Jujutsu Kaisen, Demon Slayer, and Freiren: Beyond Journey's End.
